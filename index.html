FAQ

Smarter kitchens, answered fast

Got questions? We’ve got tasty answers. Discover how our AI-powered kitchen makes meal planning, shopping, and cooking a breeze.

How does it track my food?

Just snap a photo—our AI spots what’s in your fridge or pantry, so you always know what’s on hand.

Can it help me waste less?

You bet! We recommend recipes using what you already have, so nothing gets left behind.

Does it sync with smart gadgets?

Absolutely! We’re connecting with top smart fridges and kitchen tech for effortless, guided cooking.

Is it good for special diets?

Totally! Set your dietary needs and we’ll only show recipes that fit your lifestyle.

How do shopping lists work?

We build your grocery list from your inventory and meal plan. Check off as you shop or order online.

Will I get expiry reminders?

Yes! We track expiration dates and send alerts, so you never miss a use-by date or waste food again.
