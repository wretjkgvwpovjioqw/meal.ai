FAQ

Smarter meals, zero guesswork

Got questions? We’ve got tasty answers. Here’s everything you need to know about our AI-powered meal magic.

How does it know what’s in my fridge?

Snap a pic, and our AI scans your fridge or pantry to track what you have on hand. No more mystery leftovers or forgotten veggies—just instant inventory, always up to date.

Can it help me eat healthier?

Absolutely! Our platform suggests recipes tailored to your dietary needs, preferences, and what’s actually in your kitchen. Healthy eating, made easy and fun.

Will it remind me when food’s about to expire?

Yes! We’ll nudge you before anything goes bad, so you can use up ingredients and cut down on food waste. Your wallet (and the planet) will thank you.

Can it automate my grocery shopping?

You bet. We track what’s running low and can auto-generate shopping lists or even place orders for you. Stocking up has never been this effortless.

Does it work with smart kitchen gadgets?

We’re building deep integrations with smart fridges, ovens, and more. Soon, you’ll get guided cooking, real-time inventory, and hands-free meal prep—all in one place.

Is my data safe and private?

Your privacy is our priority. All your info is encrypted and never shared without your say-so. You’re in control, always.
